# Healthcare Reimbursement & Utilization Dashboard
<h2>Description</h2>
Leveraged a large-scale public health dataset to analyze over 500k+ data points, transforming raw pharmaceutical claims into an executive-level Power BI dashboard. This project provides a "Single Source of Truth" for monitoring national drug reimbursement trends and funding distribution.
<br />
<h2>Tools Used</h2>

- <b>Power Query</b> 
- <b>Power BI</b>

<h2>Analysis walk-through:</h2>

### Business Objectives
- Evaluate the fiscal impact of Medicaid versus Non-Medicaid funding across the total reimbursement landscape.
- Identify the primary budget drivers by isolating the top 10 products contributing to national expenditure.
- Analyze regional spending variations to determine which states represent the highest financial burden.

### Data Quality
- Blank Rows: Removed empty records in key financial columns to ensure that "Total Reimbursement" and "Prescription Counts" remained accurate.
- Managing Geographic Gaps: Acknowledged a 56% "Unknown State" rate within the geographic dataset.

### Exploratory Data Analysis
- Quarterly Spend Performance: Monitoring total reimbursement and prescription volume trends.
- Concentration Analysis: Profiling the Top 10 products to understand their share of the total national budget.
